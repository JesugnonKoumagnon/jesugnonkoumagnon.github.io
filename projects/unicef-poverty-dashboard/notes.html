Multidimensional Child Poverty Dashboard — UNICEF Côte d'Ivoire

An interactive R Shiny dashboard monitoring social indicators derived from multidimensional child poverty analysis, built during a Social Policy internship at UNICEF.

Live dashboard ↗

Context

During a Social Policy internship at UNICEF Côte d'Ivoire (2024–2025), I analyzed multidimensional child poverty data and built tools to make the findings accessible to UNICEF teams and government partners.

What this dashboard does

  • Tracks social indicators related to child deprivation (health, water, sanitation, education, housing)
  • Visualizes the geographic distribution of the Multidimensional Poverty Index (MPI) and changes in deprivation across regions
  • Designed for non-technical stakeholders (government officials, program teams) to explore the data without needing R or GIS software
